Detailed Description

Perform Metropolis-Hasting algorithm on Delaunay Triangulations.

Causal Dynamical Triangulations in C++ using CGAL

Copyright (c) 2015 Adam Getchell

Performs the Metropolis-Hastings algorithm on the foliated Delaunay triangulations. For details see: M. Creutz, and B. Freedman. “A Statistical Approach to Quantum Mechanics.” Annals of Physics 132 (1981): 427–62. http://thy.phy.bnl.gov/~creutz/mypubs/pub044.pdf
